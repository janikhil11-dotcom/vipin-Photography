The Person Behind The Lens

Vipin M · Photographer

Vipin M

Photographer · Kerala, India
I am a self-taught photographer from Kerala with a deep passion for documenting the cultural soul of my homeland. My lens is drawn to the raw, the real, and the ritualistic — particularly the ancient art of Theyyam and the vibrant energy of temple festivals.

Photography, for me, is not just about capturing a moment — it is about preserving a feeling, a tradition, a story that might otherwise fade into silence.
